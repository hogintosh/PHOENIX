# Task: Defect Field ML — Fast Prediction of 3D Defect Fields

**Project folder**: `projects/20260415_DEFECT_FEILD_ML/`
**Spec**: `defect_field_ML-2.md`
**Started**: 2026-04-15

## Objective

Build a surrogate model that maps (hatch_spacing, scan_speed) → 3D defect field using:
- Step 1: 10×10 grid of PHOENIX simulations (100 training runs)
- Step 2: PCA-based autoencoder (d=2/3/4 sweep, Z-averaged 2D defect fields)
- Step 3: MLP (sklearn) mapping process params → latent PCA components
- Step 4: 20 test simulations (10 interp + 10 extrap)
- Step 5: Evaluation — field MSE, R², porosity errors

## Parameter Space

| Param | Range | Grid |
|-------|-------|------|
| hatch_spacing | 50e-6 – 150e-6 m | 10 uniform |
| scan_speed | 0.8 – 1.6 m/s | 10 uniform |

Fixed toolpath: 1mm×1mm centered in 2mm domain, 45° rotation, bidirectional.
